Common questions

Why can't I see tonight's shows?

Lineups are refreshed nightly. If no shows appear, either the venues haven't posted yet, or the last scrape hasn't run. Pull to refresh or check back in an hour.

How do favorites work?

Tap a comedian's name to mark them as a favorite, like, or skip. Sets featuring your favorites float to the top and are highlighted. Your choices are stored locally on your device and never sent to a server.

Can I get reminded before a favorite's show?

Yes. When you favorite a comedian appearing on a specific set, the app schedules an on-device reminder 1 hour before showtime. You'll be asked to allow notifications once. Reminders are purely local — no server, no tracking.

How do I share my favorites with a friend?

Tap the share icon in the top-right. The iOS share sheet lets you send a link that encodes your preferences. When your friend opens it, they'll see your favorites layered onto their own view.

How do I reset my preferences?

In the app, tap the share icon, then "Reset All" to clear favorites, likes, and skips. This only affects your own device.

Is my data private?

Yes. No account, no login, no tracking. See the privacy policy for the full breakdown.

Why isn't a venue I care about listed?

I'm adding venues as I can scrape or get access to their lineup data. Email me the venue and I'll look into it.
