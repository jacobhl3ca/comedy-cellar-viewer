Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Tonight NYC ("the app") is operated by Jacob HL as a free utility for browsing NYC comedy lineups. This page explains what data the app does and doesn't collect.

What we don't collect

  • No account, email, or login is required to use the app.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, contact info, device identifiers, location, or browsing history.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

What stays on your device

  • Favorites and ratings: the comedians you mark as favorites or skip are stored in your browser's localStorage (web) or the app's local storage (iOS). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share a preferences URL.
  • Local notifications: if you enable reminders for favorited sets, notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device by iOS. No server is involved. No push tokens are sent anywhere.
  • Theme preference: your light/dark mode choice is stored locally.

Analytics

The tonightnyc.com website uses Vercel Web Analytics and Cloudflare Web Analytics, both privacy-first services, to count aggregate page views. Neither sets cookies nor collects personally identifiable information, and individual users cannot be identified from this data. The iOS app runs no analytics at all.

External data sources

The app displays publicly available comedy lineup data fetched from venues including the Comedy Cellar. Your device fetches these listings directly from our static JSON endpoints (prebaked nightly). No identifying information is attached to those requests beyond what your browser or iOS sends by default.

Shareable preference URLs

If you tap the Share button in the app to share your favorites list, a URL is generated containing your preferences encoded in the link. This URL is only sent to recipients you choose (via the iOS share sheet or clipboard). We do not receive a copy or log these shares.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will reflect that. Material changes will be reflected in the app's App Store release notes.
